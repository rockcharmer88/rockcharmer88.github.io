Helpful Crochet Tools

These tools aren't required to get started with crochet, but they can make your projects easier, more accurate, and more enjoyable. From keeping track of your stitches to finishing your work neatly, each tool plays an important role in improving your crochet experience.

Hover over each tool to learn how it's used.

Stitch Markers

Used to mark stitches, increases/decreases, or pattern repeats to prevent mistakes.

Row Counter

Helps track rows or rounds.

Yarn Scissors

Small, sharp scissors for clean and precise cutting.

Tapestry Needle

Used for weaving in ends and sewing pieces together to finish crochet projects.
