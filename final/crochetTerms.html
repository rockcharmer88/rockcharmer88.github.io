Crochet Abbreviations Guide

Crochet patterns often use abbreviations to keep instructions short. Use this guide to understand common crochet terms.

Basic Stitches

  • ch – Chain
  • sl st – Slip Stitch
  • sc – Single Crochet
  • dc – Double Crochet

Pattern Terms

  • st(s) – Stitch(es)
  • rep – Repeat
  • sk – Skip
  • sp – Space

Common Instructions

  • yo – Yarn Over
  • inc – Increase
  • dec – Decrease
  • turn – Turn your work
